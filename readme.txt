Moolah offers an easy-to-use single-page checkout for your online business.

== Description ==

Moolah lets you sell premium goods and business services directly from your site.

The Moolah E-Commerce plugin for WordPress provides a powerful e-commerce store optimized for simple checkout. Simply enter your Store ID, login, and begin selling.  You can also load your store into non-WordPress sites.

Features:

*   Single Page Checkout keeps the customer focused on completing the purchase
*   Checkout Steps loaded dynamically using AJAX
*   Mobile friendly Order Interface can be loaded into multiple sites simultaneously
*   Centralized Management Console let's you control everything from one location


== Installation ==

Upload the Moolah plugin to your blog, activate it, then enter your Moolah Store ID.  Very simple.  You find all the information you need, and more, at http://moolah-ecommerce.com/learn/deploy .

Also, you can watch our video tutorial at http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MUeOhLBhax8 .
